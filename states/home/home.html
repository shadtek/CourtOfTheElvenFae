About Us

WHO WE ARE

Court of the Elven Fae is a character group consisting of all manner of magical folk. We enchant both children and adults alike. Come and meet us!

WE ARE DIVERSE

We have faeries, elves, fauns, unicorns, and many other original mythical or fantasy characters.

GOAL

Our goal is to add a sense of enchantment and magic to the ambiance of local charity and cultural events.

WHAT WE DO

We love to support local charity or community events by adding a bit of magic. Contact us for more information!
